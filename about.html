About ZPE ATM

IZET E-Payments Pvt Ltd is the incorporation name for ZPe ATM, founded by Duraisamy A. and managed by the other two directors. As the company’s founder and CEO, Duraisamy is responsible for setting the strategic vision and direction, while the other directors provide expertise and experience in the ever- changing financial services technology industry. Together, these individuals help ensure that ZPe ATM remains competitive and provides customers with efficient and cost-effective solutions for their financial needs.

We are a leading provider of financial services for the digital generation. Our mission is to make banking easier and more secure for everyone. We want to make sure that everyone has access to the same level of banking services, regardless of income or financial background. We offer a wide range of products and services, including ATM and mobile banking, debit and prepaid cards, loans, investments, and more. For more information about our products and services, please visit our website.

ZPE is an international bank payment and fund transfer service provider that offers cash deposit and withdrawal options. It caters to both individuals and businesses, offering a range of services that are secure and reliable. Customers have had positive reviews of the services, confirming that it is a reliable and trustworthy brand. The ZPE ATM machines are available in villages and urban locations and offer a convenient way to access your funds quickly and securely.

Welcome to the bank of ZPe. With our ZPe ATM and mobile app, you can bank anytime, anywhere. Our ZPe ATM and mobile app provide you with the convenience of 24/7 access to your accounts. With our secure technology, you can easily check your balances, transfer funds, and pay bills from the comfort of your own home. Plus, with our user-friendly interface, you can easily manage your accounts with just a few taps. Get started today and experience the power of banking in the palm of your hand!
