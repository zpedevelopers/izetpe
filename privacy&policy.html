Privacy Policy

Introduction

Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our Website, WWW.iZETPe.IN (the “Website”). By using the Website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

  1. Personal Information:
    1. Registration Data:When you create an account, we collect information such as your name, email address, phone number, and any other details you provide.
    2. Payment Information:If you make a purchase or engage in a transaction, we collect payment details such as credit card information, billing address, and other necessary financial data.
  2. Non-Personal Information:
    1. Usage Data: We collect information on how you access and use the Website, such as your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and the time and date of your visit.
    2. Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies, web beacons, and other tracking technologies to collect information about your browsing behavior on our Website.
    3. Communication Data:Any information you provide when contacting customer support or communicating with us through email, phone, or other methods.

How We Use Your Information

  1. To Provide and Maintain Our Services:
    1. To process transactions and send you related information.
    2. To manage your account, including providing customer support.
  2. To Improve Our Website:
    1. To understand how users interact with our Website and make improvements.
    2. To develop new products, services, features, and functionality.
  3. To Communicate with You:
    1. To send you updates, newsletters, marketing materials, and other information that may interest you.
    2. To respond to your inquiries, requests, and feedback.
  4. To Ensure Security and Compliance:
    1. To protect against, identify, and prevent fraud and other unlawful activity.
    2. To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms and policies.

Sharing Your Information

We do not share or sell your personal information with any third party, except in the following circumstances:

  1. With Your Consent:
    1. We may share your information with third parties if you have provided your explicit consent.
  2. For Legal Reasons:
    1. We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.
  3. With Service Providers:
    1. We may share your information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our Website and conducting our business, as long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.
  4. Business Transfers:
    1. In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your personal information may be transferred to the new owner.

Security of Your Information

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. These measures include encryption, access controls, and secure server environments. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights and Choices

  1. Access and Update Your Information:
    1. You have the right to access and update your personal information. You can do this through your account settings or by contacting us directly.
  2. Opt-Out of Marketing Communications:
    1. You can opt-out of receiving marketing communications from us by following the unsubscribe instructions included in each email or by contacting us directly.
  3. Delete Your Account:
    1. You can request to delete your account and personal information by contacting us. Please note that some information may be retained as required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

Children's Privacy

Our Website is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.
